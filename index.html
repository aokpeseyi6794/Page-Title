Lebron James known to some as the "GOAT" or "King James" was born on December 30, 1984 in his hometown Akron,Ohio. He grew up with his single mom because his father wsan't there when he was young. His family was poor and struggled raising him.Luckily his basketball coach Franky Walker took him and helped him get away from the projects. LeBron went to high school at St. Vincent - St. Mary High School in Akron, Ohio. He led his basketball team to three state titles and was named "Mr. Basketball" in Ohio for three straight years. He skipped ollege and went straight to the NBA where he was drafted number 1 pick in the 2003 NBA draft to the cleveland cavaliers.10 years later he married his highschool sweetheart, Savannah Brinson, who is now the mother of his 3 children.
